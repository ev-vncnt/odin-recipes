Pour Over Coffee

Very hipster mm yes so sophisticated

Heres what you need:

  • Hario V60
  • Matching coffee filters
  • Weighing scale
  • kettle
  • Medium Fine - Fine Coffee
  • Your favourite mug

Heres what you do with them:

  1. Heat kettle
  2. Put filter onto the V60
  3. Preheat and rinse the V60 with hot water
  4. Place on top of mug and put 15g of coffee
  5. Pour 50g of water, wait 30 seconds (bloom phase)
  6. Pour 50g, wait 10 seconds
  7. Repeat above step 3 times
  8. Wait around 3 minutes to fully brew
  9. Enjoy
