French Omelette

Description

Recreating this dish may take many years of pain and suffering. Blood, sweat and tears will be shed. But do not waver, those of you who lay eyes on these words.

Let us view the ingredients which you must acquire on your travels:

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter

Below are the necessary steps to assemble the consumable:

  1. Whisk your eggs
  2. Preheat the pan to low
  3. Melt the butter, do not brown
  4. Pour eggs
  5. Stir creating curds, lifting on and off of heat
  6. Roll once there is a cooked bottom layer
  7. Plate and garnish
